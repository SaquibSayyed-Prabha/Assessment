Assignment 13 - Sorting & Grouping

Q1. What is the purpose of sort_values()? 

> sort_values() is used to sort the rows of a DataFrame or Series based on 
one or more columns in ascending or descending order.


Q2. What is the difference between ascending and descending sorting? 

> Ascending sorting arranges values from smallest to largest (A–Z or 0–9), 
whereas descending sorting arranges values from largest to smallest (Z–A or 9–0).


Q3. What is the purpose of unique()? 

> unique() is used to return all distinct (unique) values from a column or Series.


Q4. What is the purpose of value_counts()? 

> value_counts() is used to count the number of occurrences of each unique value in a column or Series.


Q5. What is groupby()?                                          

> groupby() is used to group data based on one or more columns so that aggregate operations like sum, 
mean, count, or maximum can be performed on each group.


Q6. Write the aggregation functions available in Pandas.

> Common aggregation functions in Pandas are:
sum(), mean(), count(), min(), max(), median(),
std(), var(), first(), last()
